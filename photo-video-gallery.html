About The Office Pass (TOP)

The Office Pass (TOP) provides premium coworking and managed office solutions for startups, SMEs and enterprise teams across Gurgaon, Noida and Delhi. Designed for teams ranging from 10 to 200+ members, our workspaces combine flexibility, functionality and hospitality-led experiences to support modern businesses at every stage of growth.

With a strong presence across prime business districts and metro-connected locations, TOP offers fully furnished private offices, scalable managed offices, meeting rooms and CXO cabins, enabling companies to move in quickly and operate seamlessly.

Trusted by over 173+ companies, The Office Pass focuses on delivering consistent quality, transparent pricing and flexible terms while fostering productivity, collaboration and community.
